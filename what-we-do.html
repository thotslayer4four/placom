WHAT WE DO

Comprehensive Commodity Ecosystem Services

From farm gate to global markets — PLACOM delivers integrated services across the entire commodity value chain to empower Plateau State's agricultural sector.

OUR SERVICES

Integrated commodity marketing, supply chain management, and agro–solid minerals facilitation.

01 — Commodity Marketing

PLACOM facilitates local and international commodity trading, connecting Plateau State's agricultural producers with premium domestic and global markets. We handle aggregation, pricing, negotiation, and trade execution across multiple commodity classes.

02 — Supply Chain and Logistics Management

Building integrated, efficient supply chains from farm gate to market. Our logistics network ensures commodities move swiftly, safely, and cost-effectively through the value chain.

03 — Warehousing and Collateral Management

Modern warehouse receipt systems, temperature-controlled storage, and collateral management services that reduce post-harvest losses and unlock commodity-backed financing for farmers.

04 — Quality Certification and Grading Services

Comprehensive grading, standardization, and quality assurance systems that ensure Plateau State commodities meet national and international trade standards.

05 — Farmer Groups & Cooperative Support

Supporting the formation and growth of organized farming systems — from cooperative development to collective bargaining and shared resource management.

06 — Market Information Services

Providing real-time commodity market intelligence, price discovery tools, and data analytics to empower farmers and traders with actionable market insights.

07 — Risk Management

Price stabilization mechanisms, hedging strategies, and financial instruments that protect farmers and traders from commodity price volatility.

08 — Capacity Building and Consultancy Services

Comprehensive training programs for farmers, aggregators, artisanal miners, and commodity stakeholders — covering best practices in production, post-harvest handling, and market engagement.

09 — Strategic Business Development

Identifying new commodity markets, strategic partnerships, and growth opportunities that expand PLACOM's value chain footprint across Plateau State and beyond.

10 — Solid Minerals Facilitation

Supporting artisanal miners and mining cooperatives with structured aggregation, quality assurance, and market linkage for Plateau State's mineral resources — including tin, columbite, gemstones, and industrial minerals.

11 — Tourism & Eco-Tourism Facilitation

Showcasing Plateau State's unique near-temperate climate, natural landmarks — Shere Hills, Assop Falls, Jos Wildlife Park — and cultural heritage as economic assets, linking tourism operators to organized marketing and distribution networks.
