PLATEAU COMMODITY MARKET COMPANY LIMITED

Building the Future of Commodity Marketing and Value Chain Development in Plateau State

PLACOM is building a modern commodity ecosystem that integrates marketing, warehousing, and supply chain management to connect Plateau State's producers with domestic and global markets — driving agricultural productivity, market access, food security, industrial growth, and sustainable economic development.

ABOUT PLACOM

A State-Owned Commodity Marketing Company Driving Economic Transformation

Created by the Plateau State Government in 2024 as a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV), the company strengthens commodity marketing, warehousing, and supply chain by connecting producers and markets through structured systems that drive trade, industrial growth, and sustainable economic development.

OUR VISION

To be a continentally trusted leader in commodity marketing, warehousing, supply chain management with a view to promoting sustainable innovation and growth in the economy.

OUR MISSION

Transforming Commodity Markets Through Strategic Partnerships and Innovation

Enhancing the transformation of the global commodity marketplace through building strategic alliances and expanding market access.

WHAT WE DO

Comprehensive commodity ecosystem services.

Commodity Trading

Structured domestic, and global strategic commodity marketing.

Supply Chain & Logistics

Integrated supply chains from farm gates to global markets.

Warehousing & Collateral Management

Modern warehousing systems, aggregation centres and warehouse receipt solutions that reduce post-harvest losses and improve market efficiency.

Quality Certification & Commodity Grading

Standardization, grading and quality assurance systems that improve competitiveness and compliance with global standards.

Farmer Cooperatives & Commodity Associations

Supporting the development of organized farmer cooperatives and commodity groups for improved productivity and market participation.

Market Information System

Reliable market intelligence, commodity pricing information and data-driven decision support systems for stakeholders.

Risk Management

Promoting structured trading systems, market stabilization and sustainable commodity financing frameworks.

Capacity Building & Consultancy

Training and advisory services for farmers, cooperatives, traders, processors and commodity ecosystem participants.
